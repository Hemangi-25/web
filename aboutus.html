Crime is the deliberate performance of an act explicitly stated, prohibited, unlawful, and punished by criminal law, which is considered harmful and damaging to society. The definitions of specific crimes contained in a code must be analyzed in the context of many concepts, out of them some may not be defined in the code itself. The majority of crime is classified on the seriousness of the act or conduct. Every crime must satisfy all requirements for that particular crime to be proven guilty. If the action of a person satisfies every requirement of an offense, they are said to have committed a crime. The offense and its elements are outlined under the same criminal statute.
